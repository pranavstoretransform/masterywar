// HOW IT WORKS

Five Stages. One Killer.

Each case unfolds across five stages. New evidence, new contradictions, and one explosive twist — before you submit your final theory.

STAGE 01
Crime Discovered
The case file opens. You receive the crime description, initial suspects, and the first pieces of evidence left at the scene.
STAGE 02
New Evidence
Phone records, CCTV footage, financial logs, and witness statements surface. Study each piece — contradictions are hiding in plain sight.
STAGE 03
Interrogations
All suspects are brought in for questioning. Read their answers carefully — someone is lying, and the right question will expose them.
STAGE 04
Major Twist
An unexpected revelation changes everything. The direction of the investigation shifts — and everything you thought you knew is now in question.
STAGE 05
Final Theory
Submit your complete theory — culprit, method, motive, and the evidence that clinches it. Your score is calculated instantly.
